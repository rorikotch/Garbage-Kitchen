Broccoli Mustard Spread

One day I was making a sandwich, but I only had a few things in my pantry. Broccoli, mustard, turkey, and soy cheese. So, I decided to combine two of the ingredients- broccoli & mustard.

It might seem like a weird pairing, but it oddly worked as a sandwich spread. For this recipe I used the entire stalk of broccoli- stem to floret.

Ingredients

  • 1 Head of Broccoli
  • 1 Tablespoon of Whole Grain Mustard
  • 1 Tablespoon of Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 6 Cups of Water

Required Equipment

  • Large Pot
  • Cutting Board & Knife
  • Measuring Cups

Directions

  1. Cut the head of broccoli into florets
  2. Use a vegetable peeler or cut the tough outside off of the broccoli stalk (save the scraps for veggie broth!)
  3. Add the peeled stalk and florets into a stock-pot of boiling water for 8-10 minutes

  6. Blend until smooth, spread onto a sandwhich & enjoy!
