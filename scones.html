Coffee Ground Scones

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour (plus a more for kneading)
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar (plus two tablespoons for topping scones)
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 6 tablespoons coffee grounds
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 3/4 cup (1 & 1/2 sticks) chilled, unsalted butter, cut into 1/4-inch cubes
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup plus almond milk (sub with buttermilk or other nut milks)

Required Equipment

  • Large Mixing Bowl
  • Measuring Cups & Spoons
  • Whisky & Spatula
  • Clean Surface (for kneading)

Directions

  1. Preheat your own to 425°
  2. Line 2 cookie sheets with parchment paper or silicone mats
  3. Cut your butter in 1/4 inch cubes and set aside
  4. Whisk flour, granulated sugar, baking powder, coffee grounds, kosher salt & baking soda together in a large bowl
  5. Add butter into the large bowl and mix with your hands until the mixture looks coarse (see photo below)
  6. Whisk almond milk and vanilla in a separate bowl
  7. Add almond milk and vanilla mixture to the dry ingredients and stir until the dough starts to come together slightly (it will look shaggy)
  8. Transfer the dough to a floured surface and knead until combined
  9. Form the dough into a circle that’s around ½ inch thick
  10. Use a pizza cutter or knife to slice the dough into triangles (one batch should yield ~8 pieces)
  11. Transfer the slices onto the parchment lined baking sheets and brush with a thin coat of almond milk (each sheet should accommodate either four or five scones depending on their size)
  12. Dust the top of the scones lightly with granulated sugar
  13. Bake the scones for around 14-15 minutes or until the bottoms are slightly golden brown. Transfer to a wire rack to cool
