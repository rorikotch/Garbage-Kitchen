Ingredients

  • 12 Cups of Water
  • 2 Medium Sized Ginger Roots

Required Equipment

  • Large Pot
  • Knife & Cutting Board
  • Measuring Cups

Directions

  1. Slice the ginger into pieces (around Â¼ inch thick)
  2. Add the water & ginger to your large pot and place on high heat until the mixture boils
  3. Once the mixture boils, lower the heat to a medium flame and let the mixture simmer for around 15-20 minutes (or to taste)
  4. Allow the mixture to cool for 10 minutes. Then, strain the mixture into a pitcher, chill & enjoy hot or cold

*optional Reserve 1 cup of the boiled ginger to make candied ginger slices
