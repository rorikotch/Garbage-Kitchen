Ingredients

  • 3 Cups Old-Fashioned Oats
  • 1 Teapsoon of Kosher Salt
  • 1 Cup of Unsweetened Coconut Chips
  • 1/4 Cup of Flax Seeds
  • 1/2 Cup of Chia Seeds
  • 1/2 Cup of Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1/2 Cup of Maple Syrup
  • 1/3 Cup of Slivered Almonds (*optional)
  • 1 Cup of Dried Mango
  • 1 Cup of Dried Pineapple
  • 1/4 Cup of Canded Manadrin Orange Peels

Required Equipment

  • Large Bowl
  • Measuring Cups & Spoons
  • Rimmed Cookie Sheet
  • Silicone Mat or Parchment Paper

Directions

  1. Preheat your own to 350 degrees and line a rimmed baking sheet with either parchment paper or a silicone mat.
  2. Combine oats, kosher salt, unsweetened coconut chips, maple syrup, olive oil, chia seeds, and flax seeds in a large bowl
  3. Spread a layer of the mixture onto your rimmed baking sheet
  4. Cook for 12-15 minutes and toss the mixture halfway through the baking time
  5. Chop up the dried fruit into roughly Â¼ inch pieces
  6. Let the granola mixture cool for 2-3 minutes
  7. Add the dried fruit pieces into the granola mixture & combine the mixture
