Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of Boiled Ginger from the Ginger Tea recipe
  • 3 Cups of Reserved Water from the Ginger Tea recipe
  • 3 Cups of Granulated Sugar

Required Equipment

  • Large Pot
  • Wire Rack
  • Rimmed Baking Sheet
  • Silicone Mat or Parchment Paper

Directions

  1. Add reserved 1 cup of ginger slices from ginger tea recipe and 3 cups of water reserved from the ginger tea recipe to a large pot
  2. Pour in 3 cups of sugar and stir to combine
  3. Bring the mixture to a boil and let it boil for 45 minutes
  4. Remove the ginger slices and place them on the wire rack on top of the baking sheet linked with a silicone mat or parchment paper
  5. Let ginger cool for 30 minutes
  6. Toss in sugar & serve
