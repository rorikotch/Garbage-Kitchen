Melted Strawberry Ice Cream Cake with Fresh Strawberry Syrup

Required Equipment

  • Bundt Pan
  • Spatula
  • Knife & Cutting Board
  • Small Bowl

Ingredients

  • 1 box of cake mix of your choice
  • 1 Pint of Melted Ice Cream
  • 3 Eggs
  • 2 Cups of Confectioner's Sugar
  • 1 Teaspoon of Vanilla
  • 1/4 Cup of Milk (I used almond milk)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Cut strawberries into small pieces and set aside
  3. Pour cake mix into mixing bowl
  4. Crack 3 eggs into cake mix
  5. Pour in melted ice cream of your choice (make sure the ice cream is fully melted or the cake won’t bake through properly)
  6. Pour 1 cup of strawberries into the cake batter & reserve the other 1 cup for the glaze
  7. Mix & pour into a greased bundt pan
  8. Follow box’s baking instructions for the pan of your choice
  9. Allow time to cool on a wire rack or countertop
  10. Add the 2 cups of confectioners sugar, 1 teaspoon of vanilla, ¼ cup of milk & remaining 1 cup of strawberries into a sauce pan
  11. Heat on low until combined & smooth
