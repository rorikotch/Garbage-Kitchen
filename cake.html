Melted Strawberry Ice Cream Cake with Fresh Strawberry Syrup

Ingredients

  • 1 box of cake mix of your choice
  • 1 Pint of Melted Ice Cream
  • 3 Eggs
  • 2 Cups of Confectioner's Sugar
  • 1 Teaspoon of Vanilla
  • 1/4 Cup of Milk (I used almond milk)

Required Equipment

  • Bundt Pan
  • Spatula
  • Knife & Cutting Board
  • Small Bowl

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Cut strawberries into small pieces and set aside
  3. Pour cake mix into mixing bowl and crack 3 eggs into cake mix
  4. Pour in melted ice cream of your choice (make sure the ice cream is fully melted or the cake won’t bake through properly)
  5. Pour 1 cup of strawberries into the cake batter & reserve the other 1 cup for the glaze
  6. Mix & pour into a greased bundt pan
  7. Follow box’s baking instructions for the pan of your choice
  8. Allow time to cool on a wire rack or countertop
  9. Add the 2 cups of confectioners sugar, 1 teaspoon of vanilla, ¼ cup of milk & remaining 1 cup of strawberries into a sauce pan
  10. Heat on low until combined & smooth
  11. Pour over cake & let soak in for around 30 minutes
