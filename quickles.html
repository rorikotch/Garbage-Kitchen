Quick Pickles a.k.a. "Quickles"

I constantly have cucumbers in my fridge. They're one of my favorite snacks, I like to infuse my water with them, and I love pickles. I think they're really just the perfect vegetable. During the week I meal prep my lunches and grain bowls are always on rotation.

Usually, I top my grain bowl with some scrambled eggs, kimchi, scallions, radishes, and quickles! They add the perfect amount of acidity and freshness to the whole bowl. Sometimes, if the cucumber is slightly past its prime, I like to tear it up roughly and make this recipe as well.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of White Vinegar
  • 3 Tablespoons of Granulated Sugar
  • 2 Cups of Sliced Cucumbers
  • 1 Teaspoon Salt
  • 4 Allspice Berries (*optional)

Required Equipment

  • Mason Jar
  • Measuring Cups & Spoons
  • Knife & Cutting Board

Directions

  1. Slice the cucmbers
  2. Combine all the ingredients in the mason jar
  3. Allow the cucumbers to soak in the vinegar for at least 20 minutes, preferably overnight
