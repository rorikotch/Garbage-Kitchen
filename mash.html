Whole-Head Broccoli & Cauliflower Mash

Required Equipment

  • Large Pot
  • Cutting Board & Knife
  • Potato Masher

Ingredients

  • 1 Head of Cauliflower
  • 1 Head of Broccoli
  • 3 Tablespoons of Olive Oil
  • 3/4 Cups of Water

Directions

  1. Sauteé the broccoli and cauliflower stalks and core in 1 tablespoon of olive oil for 10 minutes on medium-high heat.
  2. Add the remaining olive oil and florets to the stalks and cook on medium heat for around 10 minutes or until tender.
  3. Add ¾ cups of water to the pot and cover for 8 minutes.
  4. Remove the pot from the heat and mash the mixture with a potato masher until the combination is the desired consistency.
